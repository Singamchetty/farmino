About Farmino

Welcome to Farmino Agri Biotech Private Limited Farmino Agri Biotech Private Limited is a pioneer in the Indian seed industry. Focused on seed research, seed production, seed conditioning, marketing and agro-allied services to serve the farming community. FABPL products (hybrids and varieties) are marketed under the “FARMINO SEEDS” brand. Committed to developing potential new hybrids and improving high yielding verities in paddy, maize, sunflower, sorghum (cereals and fodder), cereal and fodder millet, mustard, wheat and vegetable crops. The company's research, production, seed conditioning and quality assurance are supported by strong, experienced and committed teams. FABPL was established in 2003 and has completed 17 successful years in the seed business with notable milestones. So far, FABPL has marketed more than 160 products (hybrid and improved varieties) in the open field, as well as market garden crops for commercial cultivation. The company's operations are spread across 13 states of India and our products have demonstrated superior performance and are well accepted by the farming community, which has established continuous customary and commercial demand. FABPL is an ISO 9001-2015 certified company and its in-house research division is recognized by DSIR (Department of Science and Industrial Research), Ministry of Science & Technology, New Delhi.
